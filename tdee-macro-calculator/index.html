How to Use This Calculator

Enter your sex, height, weight, age, and how active you are on a typical week. Select your fitness goal (cut, maintain, or bulk) and your preferred protein intake level.

Your TDEE is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the most accurate formula for most people. Macros are then split based on your goal and protein preference, with fat set at ~25–30% of calories and the rest allocated to carbs.

Use the goal tabs to quickly compare calorie targets across all five scenarios without recalculating.
