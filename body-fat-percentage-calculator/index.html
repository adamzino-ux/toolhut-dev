How to Use This Tool

Select your preferred method and gender, then enter your measurements. The U.S. Navy Method uses body circumference measurements and is more accurate — use a flexible tape measure. The BMI Method estimates body fat from height, weight, and age alone. All calculations are done locally in your browser.
