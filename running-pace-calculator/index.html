How to Use This Tool

Pace Calculator: Enter your distance and any two of time/pace to calculate the third value. Results include speed in km/h and mph, plus your equivalent pace in both units.

Race Predictor: Input a recent race result and the tool uses the Riegel formula (T2 = T1 × (D2/D1)^1.06) to estimate your finish time for 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon distances.

Splits Planner: Set a target finish time and choose even, negative, or positive split strategy to generate a mile-by-mile or kilometer-by-kilometer pacing plan.
