How to Use This Calculator

Enter the weight you successfully lifted and the number of reps you completed in a single set. For best accuracy, use a weight you can lift for 2–10 reps — results become less reliable above 10 reps.

The calculator uses six peer-reviewed formulas (Epley, Brzycki, Lander, Lombardi, Mayhew, and O'Conner) and displays both the average and individual formula estimates. Use these numbers to program training percentages, not as a substitute for an actual 1RM attempt.

Drag the reps slider for quick adjustments without typing. Switch between pounds and kilograms using the unit toggle — your inputs convert automatically.
