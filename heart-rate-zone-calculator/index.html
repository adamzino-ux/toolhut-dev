How to Use This Calculator

Enter your age (or your measured max heart rate) and optionally your resting heart rate. Choose a max HR formula and zone method, then click Calculate My Zones.

The Karvonen method uses your heart rate reserve (max HR minus resting HR) for more personalized zones — it requires a resting HR input.

For the most accurate max HR, use a measured value from a field test rather than an age-based estimate.
